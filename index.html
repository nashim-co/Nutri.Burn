About Laboni Akter

Nutritionist and Dietitian dedicated to improving lives through evidence-based nutrition

Professional Background

I am Laboni Akter, a graduate from the Institute of Nutrition and Food Science at the University of Dhaka, currently pursuing my postgraduate studies while working as a nutritionist at the American Wellness Centre (AWC).

My goal is to make your life beautiful through science-based nutrition advice and bring new wellness to your daily routine. I pay attention to every aspect of health problems - from gastric issues to complex chronic diseases.

Currently serving at American Wellness Centre (AWC) as a nutritionist, I combine academic knowledge with practical experience to provide the best care for my clients.

My Specializations

Comprehensive nutritional guidance for various health conditions

Gastric & IBS

Proper nutrition planning to reduce stomach complications and manage Irritable Bowel Syndrome effectively.

Chronic Kidney Disease

Special dietary habits and nutritional guidance for kidney care and management of CKD.

Obesity & Diabetes

Weight control and blood sugar management through proper dietary planning and lifestyle changes.

Allergies & Skin Conditions

Dietary plans and lifestyle advice to restore skin health for conditions like vitiligo and psoriasis.

Heart Health & Hypertension

Coordination of diet and lifestyle for controlling chronic pain and blood pressure.

Women's Health (PCOS)

Proper nutrition planning for women's special problems including PCOS and anemia.
